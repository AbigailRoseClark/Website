Abigail Rose Clark

About the Artist

Abigail Rose Clark is a visual/ digital artist and photographer who loves representing the world around her in new ways. An artist of many mediums, she practices water color, pen and ink drawings, acrylic, and oil paintings. Her work focusus on the natural world and people who inhabit it. Abigail is currently a student at Georgia Institute of Technology, where she persuing a BS in Literature, Media, and Communication along with a cirtificate in Marketing. When not creating or coding, Abigail loves to hike, read, and drink coffee. She currently lives in Atlanta Georgia.
